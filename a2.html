Descendant Combinator (space)

The descendant combinator (space) matches all elements that are descendants (children, grandchildren, etc.) of a specified element.

The following example selects all p elements inside div elements:

Paragraph 1 in the div.

Paragraph 2 in the div.

Paragraph 3 in the div.

Paragraph 4. Not in a div.

Paragraph 5. Not in a div.

Child Combinator (>)

The > combinator selects all elements that are direct children of a specified element.

The following example selects all p elements that are direct children of div:

Paragraph 1 in the div.

Paragraph 2 in the div.

Paragraph 3 in the div (inside a section element).

Paragraph 4 in the div.

Paragraph 5. Not in a div.

Paragraph 6. Not in a div.
